# OmniMind Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to OmniMind ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered productivity platform.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using OmniMind, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
When you register for an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Full name
- Password (encrypted)
- Timezone preference
- Profile picture (optional)

### 2.2 Work Data
To provide our services, we may process:
- Tasks and projects you create
- Meeting transcripts and notes
- Calendar events and schedules
- Email content (with your explicit permission)
- Documents and files you upload
- Communication history with our AI assistant

### 2.3 Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- IP address and device information
- Browser type and version
- Pages visited and time spent
- Actions taken within the platform
- Error logs and performance data

### 2.4 AI Processing Data
When using AI features, we may process:
- Text content for task extraction
- Meeting transcripts for summarization
- User preferences for personalization
- Interaction patterns for improvement

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Service Provision
- To create and manage your account
- To provide AI-powered task management
- To send notifications and reminders
- To optimize your schedule and workflow
- To personalize your experience

### 3.2 AI Processing
- To extract tasks from emails and documents
- To summarize meetings and conversations
- To provide intelligent scheduling suggestions
- To generate productivity insights
- To improve our AI models (anonymized)

### 3.3 Communication
- To send important service updates
- To respond to your inquiries
- To send product announcements (opt-in)
- To deliver daily/weekly digests

### 3.4 Improvement & Analytics
- To analyze usage patterns
- To improve platform performance
- To develop new features
- To conduct security monitoring

## 4. Data Processing with AI

### 4.1 AI Service Providers
We use third-party AI services including:
- OpenAI GPT models for text processing
- Google Cloud services for speech recognition
- Claude AI for alternative processing
- Our own trained models where applicable

### 4.2 Data Sharing with AI Providers
When using AI features:
- Text content is sent to AI providers for processing
- All data is encrypted in transit
- AI providers are prohibited from using data for training
- Data is deleted from AI providers after processing
- We use enterprise agreements with data protection clauses

### 4.3 Your Control
You can:
- Disable AI features at any time
- Choose which AI provider to use
- Review what data is sent to AI services
- Delete AI-processed data

## 5. Data Storage & Security

### 5.1 Storage Locations
- Primary data center: AWS US-East-1
- Backup locations: AWS US-West-2, EU-West-1
- CDN: Cloudflare global network
- AI processing: Respective provider regions

### 5.2 Security Measures
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- AES-256 encryption at rest
- TLS 1.3 for data in transit
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Access controls and role-based permissions
- Multi-factor authentication for employees

### 5.3 Data Retention
- Account data: Retained while account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days
- Backup data: Retained for 90 days
- Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymized after 12 months
- AI processing data: Deleted immediately after processing

## 6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

### 6.1 Third-Party Service Providers
We share data with:
- Cloud infrastructure providers (AWS, Google Cloud)
- AI service providers (OpenAI, Anthropic)
- Email service providers (SendGrid, Amazon SES)
- Analytics services (Google Analytics, Mixpanel)

All third parties are GDPR-compliant and bound by data processing agreements.

### 6.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose data:
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights and property
- To prevent fraud or security issues
- To protect the safety of our users

### 6.3 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale:
- Users will be notified of any change in ownership
- Data will remain subject to this Privacy Policy
- Options for data deletion will be provided

## 7. Your Rights & Choices

### 7.1 Access & Control
You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data
- Export your data (GDPR right to portability)
- Restrict or object to processing
- Withdraw consent at any time

### 7.2 Privacy Controls
Within OmniMind, you can:
- Adjust notification preferences
- Control AI feature usage
- Manage connected accounts
- Set data retention preferences
- Enable/disable data sharing for analytics

### 7.3 Account Deletion
To delete your account:
1. Go to Settings → Security → Delete Account
2. Confirm deletion
3. All personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
4. You will receive confirmation via email

### 8.3 Opting Out

- Use incognito/private browsing mode
- Opt-out of analytics tracking in settings
- Use browser extensions for enhanced privacy
